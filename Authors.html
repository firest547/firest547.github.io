land acknowledgement

As students of UBC, we would like to acknowledge that the land on which we study is the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territory of the xwməθkwəy̓əm (Musqueam) People. As a visualization project on the land, we are recalling the unjust, rasist, and colonial practices that have had lasting legacy, and continue to create prejudiced obstacles for Indigenous peoples across Canada.
