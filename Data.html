Points of wildfires that are a basis of the historical analysis

The analysis of wildfires is based on the wildfire points shown below. These points are extracted from the National Fire Database fire data which is available in the form of polygon data and tabular point data, but have some differences as not all fires are available in the shape file. We thus use the larger point data to analyze the various aspects of wildfires.


Stations for Measurement of PM 2.5
